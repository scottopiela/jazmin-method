Check your inbox — your Day 1 lesson is waiting

Open the email from Jazmín and follow the single instruction inside. It will take less than 10 minutes and it won't involve grammar, flashcards, or vocab lists. Just your ears and a short audio.

Do this today
2
Give yourself 10 minutes a day — same time, every day

Habit formation is 80% timing, 20% willpower. Pick your 10 minutes right now — morning coffee, lunch break, before bed — and protect them. That's the only commitment this method needs.

This week
3
Don't try to speak yet — just listen

For the first 7 days, your only job is to train your ear. Spanish has 5 pure vowels. English has 15. Once your brain recalibrates, the fog lifts and words start sticking on their own. Trust the process.

Days 1–7
4
By Day 30 — a real, unscripted conversation

Not a rehearsed phrase. Not a role-play exercise. A genuine, unscripted exchange with another person in Spanish. That's the Day 30 Promise — and if it doesn't happen, you get every dollar back. No forms. No friction.
